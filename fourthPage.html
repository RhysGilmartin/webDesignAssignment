About Us

Based in Lake, RG Motors are a main dealer for two leading brands of new cars Hyundai & Mazda. We also have over 90 used cars for sale, offer car service and have a fully stocked parts department. We are located just off the first N4 junction south of Weford City.

We are proud to serve Wexford Town and the surrounding areas for almost 100 years. You will quickly discover the entire staff is knowledgeable in all things automotive.We are official dealers of Ford, Hyundai & Volvo, with an extensive range of affordable new and pre-owned vehicles, we have something for everyone.
